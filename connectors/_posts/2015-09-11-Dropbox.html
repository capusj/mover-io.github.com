We set out with a mission in early 2012 to create an easy way to move files from FTP web servers to Dropbox. Lo and behold, here it is!

Sporting one of the most advanced cloud storage systems in the universe, Dropbox has full support in Mover’s ecosystem and we’re proud to call the folks over at Dropbox our friends. They’ve created something truly game changing for the world of personal cloud storage and here at Mover we are all fans and users of Dropbox.

Looking for Dropbox for Business? We support that too. Click here!

Thousands of Mover users use our Dropbox integration for a wide variety of reasons:

  • Backing up a website into Dropbox on a regular schedule.
  • Migrating to or from Dropbox from a separate service.
  • Migrating to or from a Dropbox for Business account and an enterprise service like Box or Egnyte.
  • Sharing huge files with friends on a different service like Google Drive.
  • Creating a backup of a Dropbox account to another service like Amazon S3.
  • Saving bandwidth by copying files into your Dropbox in lieu of using the Dropbox desktop sync tool.
  • Our Dropbox Connector has bi-directional compatibility with each of our other Connectors – it is also capable of utilizing the full suite of Mover tools, like creating differential transfers, schedules and archives. The Mover Dropbox Connector is truly the best way to move files in and out of Dropbox.

Mover takes security concerns quite seriously. We take every measure to protect your files as they move in and out of Dropbox through our Connectors. That means that every transfer is encrypted end-to-end, and we are always improving our security practices.
