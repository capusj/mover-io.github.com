Mover and {{ page.html_title }}

As the world leader in file migrations, Mover is proud to support Box for enterprise file management. Box is one of the largest enterprise cloud storage providers on the planet, with a global network of datacenters and file management tools.

With our Box partnership, Mover can help you move files in and out of Box in a fast and reliable manner. There are many ways we help Box customers:

  • Migrating to or from Box from another service like Dropbox, Google Drive, or SharePoint
  • Backing up your Box account to Amazon S3, FTP, or another storage service
  • Pushing data from another service into Box on a schedule
  • Ensuring your Box account is available to your e-discovery software
  • Ensuring your Box account is SEC, FINRA, and HIPAA compliant

At Mover we take security very seriously, and that means protecting any files you move in or out of Box as tightly as we can. Every transfer is encrypted end-to-end and we are constantly improving our security practices.
