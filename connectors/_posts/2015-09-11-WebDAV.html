Mover and {{ page.html_title }}

Web Distributed Authoring and Versioning (WebDAV) is a widely used protocol for managing files between various users. It provides the framework for manipulating files through a server, and with Mover’s WebDAV Connector, this framework is now even more integrated and accessible.

Mover’s WebDAV Connector is fully set up with all other Mover Connectors, and allows for bi-directional compatibility, scheduling, archiving, and differential transfers. With the WebDAV Connector, users can now manage their files in ways such as:

  • Backing up your cloud stored files into WebDAV.
  • Migrating your WebDAV files to a cloud storage service.
  • Migrating your WebDAV files from server to server.
  • Scheduling your preferred file migrations and backups.

We at Mover take the security of your files very seriously, and we protect every file you move in and out of WebDAV as securely as we can.

Every transfer you make is encrypted end-to-end, and Mover is committed to ensuring our security practices are always being reviewed and improved upon.

With Mover’s WebDAV Connector, you will be able to manage your WebDAV files better than ever before.
