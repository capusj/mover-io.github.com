Mover and {{ page.html_title }}

Google has created a powerhouse cloud service with Google Drive!

Google Drive is an amazing cloud storage and file sharing software, and even allows for collaborative editing. Mover is thrilled to have this strong branch of Google become another strong connector of Mover!

Looking for Google Drive for Work? Click here!

The Google Drive Connector has bi-directional compatibility with all other Connectors from Mover. It is also able to utilize Mover’s full suite of tools, such as creating differential transfers, schedules, and archives.

By fully supporting Google Drive’s services with file migration management, from folders and documents to presentations and spreadsheets, we are able to provide a variety of services to customers:

  • Migrating files to or from Google Drive and a separate service.
  • Sharing large files with friends using different services such as Dropbox.
  • Backing up files to or from Google Drive from a separate service/storage device.

At Mover we take security very seriously, and that means protecting every files you move in or out of Google Drive as securely as we can. Each transfer is encrypted end-to-end and we constantly revise and improve our security practices.

Using the Mover Google Drive Connector will let you better manage your files in Google Drive.
