Mover and {{ page.html_title }}

Google is one of the leaders in enterprise cloud storage with the Google Apps suite, and specifically Drive for Work! Mover is happy to have a strong partnership with Google to offer rapid migrations and backups with Drive for Work.

Mover’s Drive for Work Connector has bi-directional migration and backup compatibility with all other Connectors from Mover, such as Dropbox, Box, Egnyte, SFTP and FTP, and Amazon S3. Our full suite of tools are availabe for Drive for Work, such as creating differential transfers, schedules, user permissions and ACL transfer.

By fully supporting Drive for Work’s services with file migration management, from folders and documents to presentations and spreadsheets, we are able to provide a variety of services to customers:

  • Migrating files to or from Drive for Work and a separate service such as Dropbox, Box, Egnyte, and Office 365.
  • Copying folder sharing and permissions when migrating in to Drive for Work.
  • Backing up files to or from Drive for Work from a separate service/storage device.
  • Many-to-many, one-to-many, and many-to-one user transfers. With permissions!
  • The fastest Drive for Work ingestion speed in the world. We fully utilize Drive for Work’s capabilities.

At Mover we take security very seriously, and that means protecting every file you move in or out of Drive for Work as securely as we can. Each transfer is encrypted end-to-end and we constantly revise and improve our security practices.

We help Google Apps partners in several ways:

  • Reduce your time to deploy Google Apps.
  • Securely migrate all your customer data from their old solution to their new Drive for Work.
  • You can self-serve via our web interface, or engage us in professional services.
  • Back up your customer’s Drive for Work to any other platform, including on-premise storage.
  • Help maintain the compliance requirements of your customer’s data.

Contact us at partners@mover.io for more information on our partner programs.
