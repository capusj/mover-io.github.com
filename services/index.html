Migrations

We use our clear, simple interface to migrate data from where it is to where it needs to be.

How it works:

  • Using our Transfer Wizard, select the source and destination cloud sites
  • Click on the folders containing the files you want transferred
  • Select if you want the transfer done now, or later
  • Kick back and relax, we’ll take it from there

Why this works:

  • Real-time assistance
  • Step-by-step instructions
  • Involves the least amount of time and personnel investment possible

Backups

Using our scheduler sets the dates and times for your backups.

How it works:

  • Using our Transfer Wizard, select source and destination storage sites
  • Select folders containing the files you want to backup
  • Input your preferred backup schedule (daily, weekly, monthly – whatever!)
  • Select the Incremental Backup option to only backup changed files
  • Go do other work – we’ve got this covered

Why this works:

  • Set it and forget it – we do all the work
  • Ensures your data is saved where it needs to be and when
  • Helps keep companies in compliance

Additional Services

File-Transfer Schedules

Need to update a large or dispersed team? Using our Scheduler, you can ensure everyone gets access to the latest information regardless of location, device or platform. Set schedules to update files across multiple platforms.

How it works:

  • This function works just like our Incremental Backups, where only new and changed files are transferred
  • We recommend you earmark a particular source specifically for team file updates; upload new materials there and our Scheduler will automatically send them out at the next update

Why this works:

  • Allows for real time updates across multiple platforms
  • Empowers staff working in multiple locations and on different devices
  • Set it and forget it – files get updated automatically

Logs

Logs are a life saver for companies with strict internal and external protocols. With this real-time service you can pull down detailed reports on your transfers including, source, destinations, plus the number and name of files transferred.

How it works:

  • Simply click the Activity tab in our Transfer Wizard
  • Review the history of all your transfers: where, when, and what
  • You can also check the status of your transfer
  • To view a detailed log of a specific transfer, simply select it and you’ll get all the details you need

Why this works:

  • It’s an essential tool for maintaining records for internal and external audits
  • If the information you manage falls under a regulator’s compliance protocols, the logs may be used to demonstrate adherence with standards
  • Saves your time and your sanity – when you need your information, it’s ready and waiting for you

Archives

Zip up your data to save space and keep your backups in order. This works best from your root directory, unless you have specific folders you have in mind!

How it works:

  • Select Archive when setting up a transfer in our app – we’ll compresses your data after downloading it.
  • We then upload a .zip folder containing all your files to your destination.
  • The archive folder will have a date and time in the file name, for easy organization.

Why this works:

  • All your files will be in one place, to give you a snapshot of a particular point in time.
  • Minimizes the amount of storage space you’re using, saving you money.
  • It’s best practice to keep your backups organized by date.
  • Later, you can sift through your archived backups and remove the ones you no longer need.
